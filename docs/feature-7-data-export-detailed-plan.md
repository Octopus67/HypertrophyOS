═══════════════════════════════════════════════
FEATURE 7: DATA EXPORT
Total Effort: 80 hours (10 days)
Dependencies: puppeteer (PDF), papaparse (CSV), boto3 (S3)
Risk Level: 🟡 Medium (GDPR compliance critical)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

## OVERVIEW

Allow users to export all their data (workouts, nutrition, measurements, photos) in machine-readable (JSON, CSV) and human-readable (PDF) formats. GDPR Article 20 compliance requirement (right to data portability).

## SUCCESS CRITERIA

- <5% export failure rate
- Exports complete within 5 minutes for 2 years of data
- 100% GDPR compliance (verified by legal team)
- Zero data leaks (exports only accessible by owner)
- All user data included (workouts, nutrition, measurements, photos, preferences)
- PDF report is professional and readable
